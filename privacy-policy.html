d2jsp Notification Notifier - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

The d2jsp Notification Notifier Chrome Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

How the Extension Works

This extension checks the user's d2jsp private message page at periodic intervals using the browser’s existing authenticated session.

0

No account credentials are accessed or stored by the extension. Authentication is handled entirely by the user’s browser session.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following locally in the user's browser:

  • Notification enabled/disabled preference
  • Sound enabled/disabled preference
  • Last known unread message count

This data never leaves the user’s device.

Third Parties

The extension does not transmit data to any external servers and does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party services.

Contact

For questions regarding this extension, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
